About


StoryMaker is a personalized story generator for children. Once information about the child pertaining to each story is entered, stories are presented for you. Not only can your child use your computer's mouse to draw on each story's page, but you also have the option to print or save each creation. We hope you enjoy StoryMaker!


This app was was created and built by Lauren Fazah, 2014.

Her mother, Marian, writes children's stories in her free time. StoryMaker is a great way for both of them to combine their passions.
